Also called தமிழர் திருவிழா[citation needed] Observed by Tamils, Malayalis Type Ethnoreligious Significance The festival commemorates the occasion when Parvati gave Murugan (aka Kartikeya) a Vel Date decided by the Tamil calendar. 2022 date Tuesday 18 January Thaipusam or Thaipoosam (Tamil: தைப்பூசம், taippūcam ?), is a festival celebrated by the Tamil community on the full moon in the Tamil month of Thai (January/February), usually coinciding with Pushya star, known as Poosam in Tamil. The festival is also observed among Keralites and is vernacularly called Thaipooyam (Malayalam: തൈപ്പൂയം).[1]
